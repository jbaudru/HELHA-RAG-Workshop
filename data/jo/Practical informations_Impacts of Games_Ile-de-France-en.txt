_UQg0t_0-en; en; What are the Olympic lanes and will they remain open to general traffic?; The Olympic lanes are the lanes used by Paris 2024 service vehicles, emergency and security vehicles, and other authorized vehicles identified in the decree.

They can be reserved and will be marked by road markings and signs.

On the Paris ring road and motorways, this refers to a lane or section of lane reserved in each direction.

The people authorised to use these lanes are those accredited by the organising committee of the 2024 Olympic and Paralympic Games. They include:

 * vehicles of persons accredited to use the Olympic lanes;

 * taxis;

 * public transport vehicles;

 * vehicles designed to facilitate the transport of people with reduced mobility;

 * and emergency and security vehicles.
   
   

All other vehicles (including bicycles and scooters) are prohibited from using these lanes. Any vehicle using an Olympic lane without prior authorisation is liable to a fine of 135 euros and legal action.
