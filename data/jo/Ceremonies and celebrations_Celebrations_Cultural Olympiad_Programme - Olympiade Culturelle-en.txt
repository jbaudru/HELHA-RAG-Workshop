OngpYmwJ-en; en; What does the Olympiade Culturelle programme mean for me?; The Olympiade Culturelle label provides the following benefits:



 * Information and promotion of a project as part of the Olympiade Culturelle's general communication plan and, in particular, visibility on the general public website

 * Participation in the dynamics and activities of the Games as a major national and international event

 * Involvement in a Olympiade Culturelle network that brings together those awarded the label, facilitating the sharing of best practices, resources, and tools

 * Presentation of certain projects from the Call for Participation or Public Call for Projects during the Olympic and Paralympic Games in Paris, Seine-Saint-Denis, or at the athletes' Village
