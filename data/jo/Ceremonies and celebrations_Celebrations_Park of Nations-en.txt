b-NRkslR-en; en; What is the Park of Nations ?; During the Paris 2024 Olympic and Paralympic Games, La Villette Park will become the Park of Nations. It will be a place of hospitality for the nations taking part in the Games, with Club France and the French sports federations taking up residence in the main hall and on the lawns.

Several National Olympic and Paralympic Committees will also take up residence in different areas of the Park. The Park of Nations will also be a place of celebration, offering sports initiation courses, concerts, etc.

Decathlon will also be present with a musical, artistic and sporting programme, meetings with Team Decathlon athletes, the broadcasting of events on giant screens, a product showroom, catering facilities, etc.
