Ongu4Gjh-en; en; What is Génération 2024?; Génération 2024 is the education and youth programme of the Paris 2024 Olympic and Paralympic Games.

It brings together the various initiatives that will contribute to the legacy of the Games.



Several components make up the Génération 2024 programme:



 * The Génération 2024 label

 * Olympic and Paralympic Week

 * 30 Minutes of Exercise Every Day

 * The Cultural Olympiad label for artistic and cultural education projects

 * "Génération 2024" Civic Services
