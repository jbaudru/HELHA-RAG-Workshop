OnhCUi7B-en; en; How can I access I'mPossible teaching resources?; I’mPossible is an educational tool produced by the International Paralympic Committee for teachers.

Divided into 3 sections (6-12 years, 12-15 years, and 15-18 years), it introduces the concepts of paralympic values, para sports, and explores inspiring journeys of athletes from around the world.



To obtain the complete resources, including presentations and student activity sheets, please send your request to the e-mail address:

generation2024@paris2024.org

Please provide your name, first name, position, institution, academy, and the purpose of the request.
