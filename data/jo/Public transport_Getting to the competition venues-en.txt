As such, all competition venues are accessible by public transportation, with an adapted transportation plan in place.

No venues in the Île-de-France region are accessible by motorized vehicles.

Alternatively, attendees can use active mobility options such as biking or walking to reach the competition sites.



For each competition venue, free monitored parking zones for bicycles and scooters are available on the day of the event.



The locations of these parking areas will be indicated in the Spectator Guides sent via email beforehand and on the pages of each competition venue.
