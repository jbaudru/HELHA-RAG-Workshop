Setup Schedule:

 * Mid-June (17 June): Start of setup on the lower quays

 * End of June (26 June): Start of setup on the upper quays

 * 8 July: Start of setup on the bridges, beginning with the Debilly Footbridge

 * Up until 14 July, setup phases will be concentrated mainly from 6 AM to 6 PM on weekdays. The quays will remain accessible to the public (tourists, athletes, residents, etc.) on weekends and in the evenings in certain areas.

 * After 14 July: The upper and lower quays will be closed to the public, with access routes maintained for residents, public establishments and their clients, and emergency and security services.
   
   

Additionally, we have collectively decided to keep four crossing bridges open until the event day, allowing passage from one bank to the other without any installations.



Dismantling Schedule:

 * 27 July - 2 August: Partial clearing of spaces to leave the Seine banks free of works during the Olympics, allowing everyone to reclaim the quays and their activities

 * 29 July: Partial reopening of the upper quays to traffic

 * 4 August: End of dismantling on the upper quays

 * 12 August - 25 August: Clearing of the entire site (except for Pont Alexandre III used for Seine events)
